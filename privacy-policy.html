Privacy Policy

Introduction

Paw Patroller UK ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you visit our website, which is hosted on GitHub Pages as a static site.

Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the site.

About Our Website

Our website is a static site hosted on GitHub Pages. This means:

  • We do not operate our own web servers
  • We do not have databases connected to this website
  • We do not process or store your data directly on our systems

Information Collection

As a static website, we collect very limited information:

Contact Information

When you contact us through phone, email, or social media links provided on our website, you may voluntarily provide information such as:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Information about your pet

This information is not collected through our website but directly through the communication channels you choose (email, phone, WhatsApp, etc.).

GitHub Pages Hosting

Our website is hosted on GitHub Pages, which may collect certain information such as:

  • IP addresses
  • Browser type and version
  • Time and date of visit
  • Pages visited

This information is collected and processed by GitHub, not by us. For more information, please refer to GitHub's Privacy Statement.

Local Storage

Our website uses browser local storage to remember your cookie preferences. This information is stored on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

Third-Party Services

Our static website uses the following third-party services that may collect data:

  • Google Fonts: We use Google Fonts to enhance the visual appearance of our website. Google may collect data about your use of these fonts. Learn more about Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Material Icons: We use Google's Material Icons, which are also subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • CDNFonts: We use CDNFonts to deliver certain font styles. Visit CDNFonts Privacy Policy for more information.
  • WhatsApp: We provide a WhatsApp button for communication. Using this feature subjects you to WhatsApp's Privacy Policy.

Cookie Policy

Our website is a static site hosted on GitHub Pages and does not use traditional cookies for analytics or marketing purposes. We only use browser local storage to remember your cookie banner preferences. This information is stored entirely on your device and is not transmitted to any servers.

The only cookies that might be set are those from third-party services we use to deliver fonts and icons, as mentioned in the "Third-Party Services" section.

How We Use Information

Any contact information voluntarily provided to us is used solely for:

  • Responding to your inquiries
  • Providing the services you request
  • Improving our website and services

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information with third parties.

Data Security

As a static website, we do not collect or store your data on our servers. However, information you share with us through other channels (e.g., email or phone) is protected with appropriate technical and organizational measures.

Your Rights Under GDPR

If you are in the European Economic Area, you have rights regarding your personal data, including:

  • The right to access data we hold about you
  • The right to have inaccurate data corrected
  • The right to have your data erased (where applicable)
  • The right to restrict processing of your data
  • The right to data portability
  • The right to object to processing

To exercise these rights regarding information you've shared with us, please contact us using the details provided below.

Children's Privacy

Our website is not intended for children under 16 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16.

Changes to This Privacy Policy
