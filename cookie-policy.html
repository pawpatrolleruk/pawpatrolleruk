How we use cookies and respect your privacy

Introduction

This website uses cookies strictly for the purpose of essential functions and to enhance your browsing experience.

Types of Cookies

Necessary Cookies: These cookies enable core functionality, such as security, network management, and accessibility. They are always active.

Non-Essential Cookies: These include Analytics and Marketing cookies. They will only be activated after you provide explicit consent.

Your Choices

When you first visit our website, you'll see a cookie banner with three options:

  • Accept All: Consents to all cookie types including analytics and marketing
  • Reject All: Only accepts necessary cookies required for website functionality
  • Manage Preferences: Opens a detailed preferences modal for granular control

You can change your preferences at any time by clicking the "Cookie Settings" link in the footer.
